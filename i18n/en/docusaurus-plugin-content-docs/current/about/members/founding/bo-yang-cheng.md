## About

Bo-Yang Cheng is the founder and director of Cheng Osteopathic & Body Sculpting Center, holding international osteopathic certification. He is also a professional fitness coach and former bodybuilding competitor, combining sports science with traditional osteopathic techniques to provide unique body alignment services.

- Sports massage
- One-on-one personal training

The center is known for its "pain-free adjustment" approach, using vibration techniques to release adhesions in spinal tissues without the typical cracking sounds associated with traditional chiropractic methods.
