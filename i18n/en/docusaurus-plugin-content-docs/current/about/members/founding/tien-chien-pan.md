# Dr. Tien-Chien Pan

![Dr. Tien-Chien Pan](/img/members/tien-chien-pan.png)

**Director, Chin-An Integrative Medicine Clinic**

## About

Dr. Tien-Chien Pan is a Chinese medicine physician who also holds Western internal medicine specialist certification. He is dedicated to combining Traditional Chinese Medicine, Western medicine, functional medicine, and other therapeutic approaches to provide comprehensive healthcare services.

## Current Positions

- Director, Chin-An Integrative Medicine Clinic
- Chairman, Taiwan Society of Orthomolecular Medicine
- Chairman, Tien-Chien International Biomedical Co., Ltd.

## Areas of Expertise

- Integrative Chinese-Western medicine
- Anti-aging therapies
- Weight management consultation
- Aesthetic medicine
- Autonomic nervous system disorders
- Menopause management

## Education

- China Medical University, School of Chinese Medicine (Dual degree: Chinese & Western Medicine)
- China Medical University, Master's in Chinese Medicine

## Experience

- Director, Pan Tien-Chien Integrative Medicine Clinic
- Director, Saint Venus Weight Management Clinic
- Chief, TCM Department, Dali Ren-Ai Hospital
- Attending Physician, Integrative Medicine, Chung Shan Medical University Hospital
- Chief Resident, Internal Medicine, Taichung Hospital
- Resident, Internal Medicine, Chi Mei Medical Center

## Professional Certifications

- Chinese Medicine Physician License
- Western Internal Medicine Specialist
- Acupuncture Specialist, Chinese Medicine Association

- Member, Taiwan Society of Aesthetic Medicine
- Member, Taiwan Society of Anti-Aging Medicine
