# Two purposes, both welcome to write us

## (a) I have a specific collaboration idea

If you already know what domain knowledge you want to bring, or have a particular AI application in mind, write to us directly.

**[Propose a collaboration](mailto:lightman.chang@gmail.com?subject=%5BProposal%5D%20My%20collaboration%20idea)** (mailto subject auto-fills with "[Proposal]")

> Reply timeframe: within 3 business days

## (b) I'd like to meet first

If you haven't decided on a specific direction yet, but want to get to know the association and chat about what we're doing, write us too. The chairman will arrange a 1:1 coffee or video call.

**[Let's meet first](mailto:lightman.chang@gmail.com?subject=%5BMeet%5D%20Want%20to%20get%20acquainted)** (mailto subject auto-fills with "[Meet]")

> Reply timeframe: within 3 business days

## Our division of labor

> **The chairman = AI integration engineer**
> **You = the expert in your field**

You contribute: years of accumulated domain knowledge, real-world cases, statutes/databases, expert review.

The chairman handles: turning these into AI tools usable by society, and maintaining the open-source repos.

**Output = [CC-BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/) public-interest projects, permanently public, contributors credited.**

---

## No fees, no membership plans

We are **completely free** — no membership fees, no paid training, no referral fees, no certification exams.

All outputs and all collaboration opportunities are open-source contributions for the public good.

---

## About physical Go meetups

The association's long-term vision is to build long-term trust through Go play.

**We have not yet started regular physical Go meetups.** If you'd like to receive notifications when we do, mention this in your email ("I'd like to receive Go meetup notifications"), and we'll let you know when meetups begin.
