# Go Events

Documentation of Go events organized and participated in by the association.

## 1st Good Go Baby Cup Family Go Carnival

**Date**: June 2024
**Venue**: Heping High School

![Carnival Venue](/img/activities/carnival/venue.jpg)

The 1st Good Go Baby Cup Family Go Carnival combined Go tournaments with family activities, allowing the whole family to enjoy the fun of Go together.

### Event Highlights

- Group Go tournaments
- Parent-child interactive games
- Go experience booths
- Awards ceremony

## 2024 Taipei Sports Federation National Go Open

**Date**: January 28, 2024
**Venue**: Penglai Elementary School

![Tournament Venue](/img/activities/events/taipei-tournament.jpg)

The association co-organized the Taipei Sports Federation National Go Open Spring Tournament, attracting players from across Taiwan.
