# Good Go Fun Club

Monthly Go social gatherings combining themed lectures, friendly games, and networking events.

## Activities

The Good Go Fun Club meets monthly in Taichung, featuring:

- **5-Minute Themed Lectures**: Expert speakers from various fields
- **1-Hour Interactive Exchange**: In-depth discussions
- **Friendly Games**: Players matching skills
- **Beginner Lessons**: 5-minute pure Go introduction

## Event Highlights

### Cross-Industry Exchange

The Good Go Fun Club is more than just a Go gathering—it's a platform for cross-industry networking. We invite speakers from diverse professional backgrounds, allowing players to broaden their horizons beyond the board.

### Precision Networking

- 5-minute presentations to understand expertise
- Continued exchange through group chats
- 1-hour deep discussions
