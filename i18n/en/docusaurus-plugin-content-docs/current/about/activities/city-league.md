# City Online League

Online city Go tournaments where players from different cities compete via the internet to bring glory to their hometowns!

## About the Tournament

The City Online League is an online team tournament organized by the Good Go Baby Association. Cities form teams to compete through the Yike Go platform.

![Yike Platform](/img/activities/city-league/platform.jpg)

### Match in Action

![Taichung Team in Action](/img/activities/city-league/taichung-team.jpg)

Players from each city gather at their local venues to compete simultaneously online, creating an exciting atmosphere.

## Tournament Format

- **Platform**: Yike Go
- **Format**: Team competition
- **Team Size**: 5 players
- **Time Control**: 10 minutes per player, 3×30 second byo-yomi
