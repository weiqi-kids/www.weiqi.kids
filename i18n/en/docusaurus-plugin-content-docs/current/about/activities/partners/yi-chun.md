# Yi-Chun Hsieh

**Professional Voice Actor**

## About

Yi-Chun Hsieh is a professional voice actor who provides voiceover services for the Good Go Baby Association's promotional videos. Her voice work spans commercials, corporate promotions, animated dubbing, and more.

## Portfolio

Yi-Chun has provided voiceovers for numerous well-known brands, including:

- Nanshan Life Insurance - Product Commercial
- Cosmed - Saeve Launch Promotion
- Pixnet - Corporate Promotion
- Hill's - Pet Food Commercial
- McDonald's - Cold Beverage Commercial
- Korean Drama "Another Oh Hae-young" - Chinese Dubbing

## Collaboration

Provides professional voiceover services for the Good Go Baby Association's promotional videos and event introductions, conveying the charm of Go culture with a warm and friendly voice.
