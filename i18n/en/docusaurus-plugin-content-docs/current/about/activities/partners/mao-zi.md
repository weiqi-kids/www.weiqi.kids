# Mao Zi

**Illustrator**

## About

Mao Zi is an illustrator who created a series of Go-themed wallpapers for the Good Go Baby Association, integrating Go elements into daily life through a dreamy artistic style.

## Collaborative Works

### Go-Themed Wallpapers

![Go-themed Wallpaper](/img/partners/mao-zi-wallpaper.png)

Phone wallpapers featuring stars and Go stones as the theme, with black and white stones transformed into cute planets floating in space.

## Art Style

- Dreamy, ethereal illustration style
- Combination of Go elements with natural scenery
- Available in both mobile and desktop versions
- Monthly theme updates
