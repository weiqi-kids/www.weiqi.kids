# Wu Di

**Illustrator**

## About

Wu Di is a professional illustrator who designed multiple adorable mascot characters for the Good Go Baby Association, including Dora (fox), Yabo (raccoon dog), and special Dragon and Tiger characters for the Lunar New Year.

## Collaborative Works

### Character Design: Dora & Yabo

![Dora](/img/partners/wu-di-dora.png)

**Dora** is a fox character wearing traditional Japanese clothing, with a lively and cute personality.

### Zodiac Character Design

![Dragon and Tiger](/img/partners/wu-di-dragon.jpg)

Special Dragon Year and Tiger Year characters designed for Good Go Baby, blending Go elements with traditional culture.
