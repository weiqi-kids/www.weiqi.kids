# Introduction

Welcome to the world of Go! This section will take you from zero to building a solid foundation in Go.

## Contents of This Section

### Go Rules

The rules of Go are simple yet profound. You will learn:

- **Liberties**: The lifeline of stones
- **Capturing**: How to capture opponent's stones
- **Illegal moves**: Places where you cannot play
- **Ko**: Special repetition rules
- **Eyes and living groups**: How to keep groups alive
- **Scoring**: Territory counting and area counting methods

[Start learning Go rules →](./rules)

### Basic Terminology

Learn Go terminology so you can:

- Understand game commentaries
- Communicate with other players
- Follow teaching content

[Learn Go terminology →](./terminology)

### Game Etiquette

Go is not just about winning and losing, but also a culture. Learn:

- Etiquette when placing stones
- Attitude during games
- Grace in victory and defeat

[Understand game etiquette →](./etiquette)

### Opening Concepts

Before starting to play, establish the right concepts:

- General direction of the opening
- Value of corners, sides, and center
- Characteristics of common opening positions

[Learn opening concepts →](./first-10-moves)

## Learning Suggestions

:::tip For Beginners
Don't rush to win games. When first learning Go, the most important things are:

1. **Truly understand the rules** - Especially the concepts of "liberties" and "eyes"
2. **Play more games** - Practice on the 9x9 small board to accumulate experience
3. **Enjoy the process** - Go is a lifelong interest, take your time
:::
