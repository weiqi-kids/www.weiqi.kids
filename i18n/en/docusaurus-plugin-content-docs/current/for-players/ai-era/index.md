# The AI Era

In 2016, AlphaGo defeated world champion Lee Sedol, shocking the entire Go world. From that moment, Go entered a completely new AI era.

## The Significance of This Era

Artificial intelligence didn't just defeat human players - more importantly:

- **Broke existing understanding**: Many "common knowledge" concepts were overturned
- **Opened new playing styles**: Discovered moves humans never imagined
- **Changed learning methods**: AI became a powerful learning tool

## Impact on Amateur Players

The emergence of AI presents both opportunities and challenges for amateur players:

### Opportunities

- Can play against top-level opponents anytime
- Can analyze your games in real-time
- Learning resources have become much richer

### Challenges

- How to properly use AI tools
- Avoiding over-dependence on AI
- Maintaining the joy of playing in the AI era

:::tip Keep Your Original Intent
In the AI era, we have unprecedented learning tools. But remember: the joy of Go lies in the process of thinking, the interaction of playing, and the happiness of improvement.

AI is a tool, not the goal. Use it well, but don't let it take away the joy of playing.
