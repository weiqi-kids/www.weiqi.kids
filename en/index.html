Origin Story

Why Go?

We are "the driving force behind Weiqi culture" — because the Go community was the first to feel the AI wave.

When AlphaGo defeated Lee Sedol in 2016, Go players were among the earliest to be transformed by — and to embrace — artificial intelligence. Today, we carry that experience into health, industry, education, and public policy, helping more people benefit from this technological tide.
